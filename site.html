  1. Generale information about places to visite in tunisia

    1. Carthage: Explore the ancient ruins of Carthage, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The archaeological site includes remains of Roman baths, theaters, and the famous Carthage Museum.
    2. Tunis Medina:
      The historic center of Tunis is a UNESCO-listed site, known for its narrow winding streets, vibrant markets, and traditional architecture. The Zitouna Mosque is a prominent landmark in the Medina.
    3. Dougga: Another UNESCO World Heritage Site, Dougga is an exceptionally well-preserved Roman city with impressive temples, theaters, and a mausoleum.
      4. Sidi Bou Said: This picturesque coastal town is famous for its blue and white architecture. Visitors can stroll through narrow streets, enjoy panoramic views of the Mediterranean, and visit the House of Baron d'Erlanger.
